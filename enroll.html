Verify Eligibility Ensure You Meet The Following Eligibility Criteria:

You must be a Resident of Bangalore Urban, Bangalore Rural & Ramanagara District.
You should have Graduated Before November 2020.
Your convocation should belong to an Indian-Approved University by the State Government.
You must have a Government Approved Address Proof of Bangalore Urban, Bangalore Rural & Ramanagara District.

Download Form 18

You can download Form 18 from the official election commission website ( https://eci.gov.in ) or Please refer to the PDF Sent below.

Fill in Form 18 Accurately

Complete all the required fields in Form 18. Provide accurate information and double-check for any errors.

Attach the Government Approved Address Proof to the Form 18

  • Voter Id
  • Aadhar Card
  • Ration Card
  • Indian Passport
  • Driving License
  • Bank Passbook

Attach Your Convocation or All Semesters Marks Cards

Make a photocopy (Xerox) of your Convocation Certificate or All Your Semester Marks Cards as proof of your graduation.

Attach 2 Passport Size Photos to the Form 18.

Staple and Sign all the 3 documents Including.

( Filled Form 18 + Government Approved Address Proof + Convocation Certificate or all Semester Marks Cards + 2 Passport Size Photos ).
Sign on all the sheets / Documents as required.

Get the Gazetted Officer Signature

Submission

Take the completed and signed Form 18 along with attached documents to your nearest BBMP Office or Tahsildar Office. Submit your application in person.
