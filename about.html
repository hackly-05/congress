Anekal Ramoji Gowda

Bangalore Graduates Constituency Aspirant

Greetings to the Voters of Bengaluru Graduates' Constituency

Soon elections for the Bengaluru Graduates MLC Constituency will commence and I am currently working in the constituency as an aspirant for a ticket from the Congress party.

The contribution of the representatives of the graduates' constituency in the Vidhan Parishad is known to those who have trusted and voted for them.

During my 14-years of experience as a Teacher, I had encountered a lot of problems faced by employees in all sectors including teachers and graduates and formed many organisations to address these issues.

I have served as the District President of the Bengaluru South District Teachers Association, Vice President of the State Teachers Association, Vice President of the KPCC State Graduates Wing, Vice President of State Government Employees Association, and President of the Karnataka State Graduates' Forum.

I had the honour of working as the coordinator of the Bharat Jodo Yatra led by Congress leader Sri Rahul Gandhi. I am also serving as the Founder President of the Bangalore City District Teachers Co-operative Society.

  • I have established 25 coaching centres for SSLC, PUC, CACPT, Medical Foundations, CET, NET, etc., to help students.
  • It brings me satisfaction that the Britain International School has opened in Magadi Road. Provided employment to many teachers.
  • I have made a comprehensive plan to provide housing for these teachers and employees at a special discount and housed hundreds of people.
  • Through NexPlace Info Pvt. Ltd, I have organized numerous job fairs and successfully facilitated the employment of unemployed individuals by matching them with suitable job opportunities in hundreds of companies, taking into account their qualifications requirements.
  • I have actively organized blood camps and health camps, providing essential healthcare services to those who were in need. In addition, I have played a crucial role in distributing free medicines, spectacles, and other necessary articles to patients, ensuring they receive the support and resources required for their well-being.
  • I have personally undertaken the responsibility of adopting the Government Senior Primary School located in Koramangala -Jakkasandra, and I have allocated a budget of 80 lakhs to enhance and improve the school's facilities.
  • The welfare of the people has been my motive in doing all this work and this brings great pleasure. I promise to continue working in the same manner throughout my life.
  • If given the opportunity, I shall act as the voice of the people to work for the welfare of the graduates, teachers and unemployed, and bring an era of social, economic and cultural empowerment and sustainable development. This is my heartfelt pledge.
  • Despite contesting as the Congress candidate from the constituency in 2012 and 2018, I have lost by a narrow margin. However, considering failure as a stepping stone to success, I have been determined to continue my work.
  • With your support, cooperation and guidance, I will be able to bring substantial changes in the graduates field. I hereby promise that I will keep up trust and faith in me.
